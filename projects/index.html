School

  • Seller Strategies and Price Dynamics on Amazon Marketplace: My undergraduate economics thesis. It characterizes distinct groups of Amazon Marketplace sellers along multiple dimensions of pricing strategy. 2011-2012

  • Sparse Coding and an Application to Topic Modeling: My master's statistics thesis. It describes an implementation of sparse coding for topic modeling, as well as theoretical results for the procedure. 2011-2012

  • Markov Chain Monte Carlo: A brief introduction to the theory and practice of Markov Chain Monte Carlo methods, written as part of the UChicago Math REU program. Summer 2010
